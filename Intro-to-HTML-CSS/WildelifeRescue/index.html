Welcome to Wild Rescue. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment. We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured wildlife.

Our staff provides around-the-clock care for wildlife in need. We also provide longterm care for wildlife that cannot be released back into their natural environment.

Will you partner with us? We need volunteers to help care for our animals. You can also donate, become a member, or become a sponsor. Contact us today.
